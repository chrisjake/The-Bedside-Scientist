#Epidural Catheter

Epidural catheters are commonly used for:
* Analgesia
    * Major abdominal or thoracic surgery  
    In combination with a general anaesthetic.
    * Labor
    * Chronic pain
* Anaesthesia
    * As a sole technique, e.g. epidural top-up
    * As a combined spinal-epidural technique
