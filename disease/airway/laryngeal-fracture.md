#Laryngeal Fracture



##Clinical Features
* May present with **acute airway obstruction**  
* 'Classical triad' of;
	* Hoarseness
	* Subcutaneous emphysema
	* Palpabl fracture

##Management
* Intubation  
Indicated by complete obstruction or severe respiratory distress.
* Surgical airway is second-line
