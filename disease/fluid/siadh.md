#Syndrome of Inappropriate ADH

SIADH is a clinical syndrome defined by excessive secretion of ADH.

##Epidemiology and Risk Factors
##Pathophysiology
Causes of SIADH can be classified into:
* Primary CNS
	* CVA
	* Haemorrhage
	* Infection
	* Trauma
	* TBI
	* Psychosis
* Respiratory
	* Pneumonia
	* Asthma
	* Atelectasis
	* Acute respiratory failure
	* Pneumothorax
* Hormonal
	* Hypothyroidism
	* Hypopituitarism
* Malignancy
	* Paraneoplastic syndrome  
	Clasically small cell lung carcinoma.
	* Neuroblastoma
* Infective
	* HIV
* Congenital
* Surgery
* Drugs
* Exercise associated
* Primary polydipsia
* Idiopathic
